OUR STORY

Jordyn and Martin met through a friend of a friend during lunch break in their first few weeks at SAIT. After first meeting they began to meet every day for lunch and had many long study sessions. Though it wasn’t until their friends cancelled on them last minute at the movies and they were left alone to chat that Martin asked Jordyn on their first date. To this day their friends still say this wasn’t a set up. Flash forwards to September of 2021 when on a hike, amidst a camping trip with friends, Martin got down on one knee and asked Jordyn to marry him. They can't wait to have their next adventure with all of you in Canmore later this year!
